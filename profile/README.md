## What we do

KiniunCorp focuses on improving how software is designed, built, and executed in the age of AI.

We build tools and systems that add:

- structure to AI-assisted workflows  
- clarity to engineering decisions  
- reliability to execution  
- traceability across the development lifecycle  

Our goal is simple:  
**turn AI from a powerful assistant into a dependable engineering system.**

---

## Projects

### Spec-To-Ship (`s2s`)

A structured engineering workflow for AI-driven development.

Spec-To-Ship turns raw AI chat sessions into a real software process — with stages, approvals, and persistent state — while remaining fully chat-native and tool-agnostic.

- Works with Claude Code, Codex, OpenCode  
- No lock-in, no platform dependency  
- Designed for real-world engineering workflows  

👉 https://github.com/KiniunCorp/spec-to-ship

## Philosophy

We believe:

- AI is powerful, but unstructured workflows create risk  
- Engineering requires process, not just output  
- Tools should remain simple, transparent, and composable  
- Open source should be genuinely useful — not crippleware  

Our work reflects these principles.

---

## Open source

Our projects are built as real open-source software.

- No core functionality is paywalled  
- No artificial limitations between free and paid versions  
- Sponsorship supports development — it does not unlock features  

If our work is useful to you or your team, consider supporting the projects directly.
